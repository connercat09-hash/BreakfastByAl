Our Story

Meet Al, the man behind the griddle

Breakfast by Al started with a simple idea: the first meal of the day deserves to be cooked fresh, not sitting under a heat lamp. From his truck in Newport News, Al makes every sandwich, burrito, and omelet to order — fried-hard eggs, hickory-smoked bacon, and toasted burillo rolls, plus turkey options across the menu.

As a locally owned small business, Breakfast by Al is built on the community it serves. Great food, honest prices, and friendly service, every single day the window is open.
