About Big Mama

Big Mama's Cookie Jar started the way all the best things do — in a family kitchen, with flour on the counter and somebody asking for "just one more." What began as baking for the people she loves grew into a mission: to cater to every sweet tooth and bring happiness to every household and kitchen across Virginia.

Every recipe is made from scratch, in small batches, on Fridays and Saturdays only — because fresh isn't a buzzword here, it's the whole point. When you order from Big Mama, you're not just getting dessert. You're getting a seat at the family table.

FAQ

When are orders baked?

Fresh orders are prepared every Friday and Saturday to ensure the highest quality and homemade experience. Place your order during the week and it'll be baked fresh on the next bake day.

Where do you deliver and ship?

Local pickup and delivery are available, and we ship throughout Virginia. Large catering and event orders are available upon request anywhere in the Commonwealth.

Do your desserts contain allergens?

All products may contain common allergens, including eggs, dairy, and wheat. If you have questions about a specific treat, send Big Mama a message before ordering.

Can I order for a wedding or big event?

Absolutely — that's one of Big Mama's favorite things to do. Use the Large Orders form and she'll build a sweet spread for your day.

How should I store my treats?

Cookies stay soft in an airtight container (or a cookie jar!) for several days. Nana Puddin' lives in the fridge and is best enjoyed within 3 days — if it lasts that long.
